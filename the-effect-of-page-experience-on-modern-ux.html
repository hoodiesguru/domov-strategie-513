The Effect of Page Experience on Modern UX

Publikoval Zdeněk Dvořák | 08. 07. 2026 | ⏰ 1 min čtení

Google's move towards UX metrics means that load times, rendering smoothness, and interactivity are essential. These metrics track LCP, FID, and CLS.

Static sites natively excel at these metrics by serving pre-built HTML. This technical edge allows digital marketing assets to naturally outrank slower, dynamic CMS frameworks.
